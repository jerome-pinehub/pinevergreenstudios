Business Operation's Mission

Our goal is to provide T. Rowe Price with the most efficient, cost-effective resources at its disposal to meet and exceed current and future enterprise-wide customer needs and expectations.

Our Role

We offer services in information product administration, finance, print production, fulfillment, process and service development and digital print services. We work closely with our customers toward a common goal of producing the highest-quality product. Our strategies include applying quality-control standards (for ourselves and our vendors), maximizing cost and labor efficiencies through effective vendor management, and continually developing new technologies, products, and services to ensure we are meeting the changing needs of our customers. Through our production teams we offer a comprehensive range of services.

Our Strategy

The Business Operations group serves the entire Enterprise Publishing department through the following activities:

  • Serving as a liaison between clients and the department.
  • Measuring customer satisfaction and facilitating solutions to enhance satisfaction.
  • Communicating the department’s contributions to the firm’s overall success.
  • Establishing an identity for Electronic Publishing.
  • Advocating for internal clients and the department.

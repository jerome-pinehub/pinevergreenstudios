Our goal here is to rate, review and recommend appropriate programming for children and youth on the television, radio, and video games. We have a seven point rating system which we use to complete our charts.

The reviews pertaining to the content of the movies, radio format and gaming are as such:
1) Violence
2) Language
3) Sexual Content
4) Drug Use
5) Witchcraft
6) Defamations of Gender
7) Message Content
